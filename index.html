Certificate in Data Science (CDS)

Data science is not the future. It is here and now! Don’t get left behind!

We have created this course in response to the growing demand for data scientists around the world. Data science is a blend of statistics, mathematics, computer science, machine learning and programming.CDS is course is distinctive because it focus on all the necessary foundations and the required skills to make a career in data science. Our students are trained in a combination of all these subjects to solve real life industry data science problems. We heavily emphasize on hands-on programming of the concepts taught in the class which makes the student industry ready from day-one for a lucrative career in data science in the industry.

World Class Data Science Training

Our instructors are from MIT, Harvard, University of British Columbia, Indian Statistical Institute (ISI Kolkata), IIT Kanpur with solid industry experience in data intensive, quantitative industries in USA, Canada and India. Each of our instructors is capable of teaching hand-on programming along with mathematics, statistics, computer science, algorithms and large-scale data processing architectures such as Spark.

Your CDS journey

For your data science journey, we tailor the course specific to your needs based on your current level of knowledge. We will provide you with one-to-one training on programming, careful feedback on your home-assignments, and assist you in completing the assignments for all modules. Moreover, we will assist you even if you don’t know where to start, or debug your code or how to complete it. Our course material has been designed to equip you with the latest and highly sought after machine learning and AI knowledge of the following module types.

    Machine learning & AI methods
  • Programming with machine learning libraries
  • Machine learning with Bigdata: Spark
  • Numerical methods for machine learning
  • Mathematics and statistical methods in machine learning
  • Industry oriented capstone project

To complete the CDS participants will have to carry out an industry level projects, in data science and machine learning applied to industry based on the learning during the course. Our instructors will help you in every stage of the project for final submission and completion of the CDS course.

Getting into the program

The CDS program is an open-ended program and comprises of many modules, stressing on various aspects of data science, depending on what kind of role you are targeting. To give our students individual attention we limit the class size.
